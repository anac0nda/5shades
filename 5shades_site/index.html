About the company

SafeOps is an industrial connectivity company focused on private 5G deployments for warehouses and industrial sites in France and Europe. We combine telecom-grade engineering with a warehouse-first delivery model.

What we believe

  • Connectivity is a production system — measurable and reliable.
  • Adoption depends on operational acceptability, not only technical specs.
  • Private 5G should be packaged as outcomes, not infrastructure.

How we work

  • Discovery → site survey → pilot → rollout
  • Clear KPIs (coverage, downtime, device performance)
  • Security review aligned with IT/OT governance
  • Managed service with SLAs and change control
